About Airport Lost & Found

Our Mission

The Airport Lost & Found system is designed to help travelers recover their lost belongings quickly and securely. Using advanced hash-based tracking technology, we ensure that every item is uniquely identified and stored safely.

Technology

Our system employs cryptographic hashing algorithms to generate unique identifiers for each reported item. This ensures data integrity and prevents unauthorized access to sensitive information.
